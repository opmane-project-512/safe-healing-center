Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer payment plans?

We offer flexible payment options including monthly and yearly subscriptions. We also accept HSA and FSA payments for eligible services.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. All our pricing is transparent. The only additional costs might be for specialized herbal formulations or premium add-on services.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for seniors or students?

Yes, we offer 15% discounts for seniors (65+) and students with valid ID. Military personnel and first responders receive 20% off all services.

What if I need to miss an appointment?

We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. Subscription members can reschedule without penalty, while individual service bookings may incur a fee for late cancellations.
